Fitness Plans

With our fitness plans, we offer tailored made sessions based on your dogs capabilities and needs.

Whether your dog is old, recovering from an injury or is just lacking in energy, we can provide a slow paced, easy going session to give your friend the necessary exercise without overwheming them.

Or perhaps your dog is full of energy and you are looking for a fast paced, agility session that not only meets their energitic needs, but stimulates their mind with fun and playful games and agility track courses.

Or maybe you are just looking to have some fun with your best friend, we can provide a fitness plan for you.


Benefits


Endurence

Burns fat, strengthens the heart and lungs, and is a great natural stress reliever for dogs.

Strength

Stimulates muscle growth, increases bone density, and strengthens connective tissues that can help prevent injuries.

Balance

Builds better body awareness in dogs and takes stress off the weaker limbs to maximize proper posture and development.

Flexibility

Promotes better range of motion, better circulation, and more oxygen to the muscles.

Mental

Mental training builds cognitive abilities and beats boredom and destructive behavior.

Quality of Life

Because a fit dog is a happy, healthy, calm, and disciplined dog!
